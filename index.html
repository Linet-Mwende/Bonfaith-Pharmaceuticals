★ About us ★

Bonfaith Pharmaceuticals was started by local area pharmacists to curb the lack of required drugs. It occurred to us how residents’ diseases accentuated in the chaos of looking for medicine or gathering enough pennies for them.

We acquire and supply quality drugs at fair prices improving community health.
